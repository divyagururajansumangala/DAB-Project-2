Airplane Crash EDA

Crash Report

The project kicked-off with an intention to analyze the available data of airline crashes from 1908 till 2019.

Kaggle for data set - 4000+ records


Hypothesis:-

  • Is there any link between the different time periods and the fatality rate?

  • What has been the overall trend for air travel fatalities?

  • Which Aircraft types were most represented in accidents?

  • What are the most dangerous air operators?

  • Which countries had the most accidents?

Visualization techniques

Python Flask-powered API Endpoints created with server-side rendering.
HTML, CSS, Python,Plotly ,Geopy ,Apex.js,Vanilla JavaScript and D3.js used in combination for creating visuals including graphs and charts.


Conclusion

  1. Initilaly Fatalities respective to the crashes were low ,then sky rocketed during era world war. Later towards end of 21'st century number of crashes plummeted.
  2. Number of crashes over the period has increased.But majority of the crashes happend between 1948 and 1970.
  3. Aircraft types caused most fatalities- Douglas Dc-3; followed by De Havilland Canada.
  4. Top dangerous air operators are Aeroflot,Miltary- US Air Force and Air France.
  5. United States consitute of 35.7% of total crashes.
